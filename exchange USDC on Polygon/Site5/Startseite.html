How to exchange USDC on Polygon?

Wether to buy a Dollia Gene NFT or to purchase DLST Tokens ​requires USDC settlement. 

In response to the user's request, we produced this tutorial, which also works for other currency exchanges on Polygon.

A Quick Know

Polygon is an excellent blockchain solution for Layer2. GAS costs are extremely low due to PoS based Ethereum layer 2 network. Customers need to use the native MATIC as a GAS fee to deliver their daily business. Therefore, switching from ETH to Polygon can maintain the value consistency of various major cryptocurrencies, which are based on ERC20.

3 Steps

connect wallet
switch network
exchange

Step 1

Log in and connect to the wallet, and click Network

Step 2

Click Add Network

Enter in the Matic Mainnet settings as follows:
Network Name: Polygon
New RPC URL: https://polygon-rpc.comChainID: 137
Symbol: MATIC
Block Explorer URL: https://polygonscan.com/

Polygon provides a web wallet service, visit https://wallet.polygon.technology. and click "Connect to a Wallet"

Choose your Metamask wallet.

Click "Next".

Click "Sign".

Click "Connect"

You have already connect your Polygon web Wallet to your Metamask Wallet!

Step 3

Use Polygon Bridge to swap your cryptocurrencies to Plygon.

Click Polygon Bridge.

Select the currency you want to exchange, here in Dollia Project, please choose USDC.

You will be prompted to sign and approve the transaction in your MetaMask wallet which is where the gas fee will also be quoted, and you will see a progress tab bar indicating when your transfer is complete.

Note that the Proof of Stake (PoS) bridge is secured by validators and will take approximately 3 hours when/if you want to move your funds back to Ethereum. 

be aware

You need to exchange GAS Token, which is in the "Swap for GAS Token" service of the wallet. You can exchange part of the transferred cryptocurrency for MATICs.
